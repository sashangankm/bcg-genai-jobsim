# Boston Consulting Group (BCG) GenAI Job Simulation (Forage)

This repository contains my work from the **BCG GenAI Consulting Job Simulation** hosted on [Forage](https://www.theforage.com/).

The program simulated real-world consulting scenarios where I acted as a Junior Data Scientist on BCG's GenAI consulting team. The primary objective of the simulation was to develop an **AI-powered chatbot capable of analyzing financial documents**, applying technical expertise in artificial intelligence and data analysis within the financial domain.

---

## 📂 Tasks Completed

### [Task 1 – Data Extraction and Initial Analysis](https://github.com/sashangankm/bcg-genai-job-simulation/tree/main/Task-01-Data-Extraction)

* Extracted key financial data and performance metrics from standard 10-K financial documents.
* Conducted foundational analysis to identify significant financial trends and indicators.
* Cleaned, formatted, and processed the extracted data to prepare it for integration with an AI model.

### [Task 2 – Developing an AI-Powered Financial Chatbot](https://github.com/sashangankm/bcg-genai-job-simulation/tree/main/Task-02-Financial-Chatbot)

* Developed an AI chatbot leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) principles to analyze financial data.
* Integrated the previously extracted and cleaned 10-K data into the chatbot system.
* Tested the chatbot to ensure it could effectively and accurately communicate complex financial performance insights and comparisons interactively.

---

## 🛠️ Skills Demonstrated

* **Generative AI & NLP** – Chatbot development, Natural Language Processing, and transforming raw data into interactive insights.
* **Data Engineering & Analysis** – Extracting, cleaning, and formatting complex data from 10-K financial statements.
* **Python** – Data manipulation, processing, and AI system integration.
* **Problem-Solving in Consulting Contexts** – Bridging the intersection of finance and AI to deliver client-ready tools.
